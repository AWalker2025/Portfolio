A Little Bit About Me!

I am an upcoming graphic designer and web developer who believes in helping clients take their ideas and bring them to life!

I am currently attending Clarkson University, pursuing a B.S degree in Communication, Media, and Design with a minor in Business, focusing on web development, logo design, and digital art. I was introduced to the magical world of graphic design by watching my mom, who has owned her own sign business for 40+ years.

During my sophomore year of college, I participated in an internship with Clarkson’s University Office of Development and Alumni Relations for their annual Athletic’s Giving Day, in which I helped raise nearly 300k to help support our athletic teams on campus.

I currently live in New York and my free time is spent with my significant other, out on the ski slopes of my local ski mountain, and with my 3 Australian Shepards.
